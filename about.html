Who We Are

Unveiling the Authenticity of Sri Lanka

Ceylon Peacock Travel was born out of a deep love for this serendipitous island. We believe that travel is not just about visiting places, but about feeling, tasting, and living the culture.

Our team consists of certified guides and travel enthusiasts who have explored every corner of Sri Lanka. From the misty mountains of Ella to the golden beaches of Mirissa, we craft experiences that are personal, sustainable, and unforgettable.
